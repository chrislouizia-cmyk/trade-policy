# The Trade Police Constitution — Version 1.0

## Mission

**Protect disciplined traders.**

Trade Police does not exist to sell signals, encourage more trading, or replace the trader. It helps traders execute their own strategy with consistency, discipline, and confidence.

## Motto

**No trade without evidence.**

## First Principle

> Trade Police will never grow by adding random features. It will grow by becoming better at one thing: protecting disciplined traders.

Every feature must protect capital, reduce impulsive decisions, improve execution, improve consistency, improve learning, increase trust, or reduce friction.

## Values

### Protect
Protect capital, discipline, consistency, privacy, and trust.

### Explain
Every authorization, rejection, recommendation, and intervention must explain why.

### Simplify
Complexity belongs inside the system, never inside the trader's head.

### Earn trust
Reliability, security, predictability, and transparency are product features.

### Improve
Every release should make the trader better—not merely make the software larger.

## Product Promise

Trade Police rewards process, not luck. A perfectly executed losing trade can be disciplined. A profitable rule violation remains a violation.

## Security Promise

Customer strategies, trades, screenshots, notes, and personal information belong to the customer. Staff receive only the minimum access required for their role. Privileged actions are individual, auditable, revocable, and protected by MFA.

## Release Standard

We do not ship when confusion can cause a costly mistake. Before release, the core workflow, balance integrity, strategy enforcement, permissions, mobile experience, and customer-facing errors must be verified.
