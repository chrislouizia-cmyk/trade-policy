# Trade Police v13 — Organizations & Staff Workspaces

## Included

- Trade Police HQ naming and navigation.
- Internal organization foundation for future white-label and partner firms.
- Individual staff accounts—never shared credentials.
- Roles: Owner, Head of Sales, Compliance Officer, Support, Technician and Security Admin.
- Granular permission bundles.
- Role-specific workspaces.
- Owner staff directory and assignment by existing account email.
- Staff suspension and restoration.
- Audit records for role assignment and status changes.
- Feature flag and release-note foundations.
- Trade Police Constitution and Manifesto.

## Privacy boundary

Staff dashboards intentionally do not expose customer strategy rules, screenshots, private notes, credentials or full trade details.
