# v12 — Operation Blue Shield

This release is a focused pre-beta UX polish built on v11.1.

- Greeting now appears before primary navigation.
- Trading account and strategy context is more compact.
- Persistent Trade Police Shield communicates readiness.
- Dashboard metric cards are smaller and easier to scan.
- Setup checklist disappears after all three onboarding steps are complete.
- Market analysis now shows staged progress rather than a generic loading message.
- Provider-specific technical names are hidden from the customer analysis summary.
- Release notes are visible on the dashboard.
- Product Decision Log added to protect the product vision.
