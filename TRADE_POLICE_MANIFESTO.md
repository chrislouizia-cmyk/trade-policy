# Trade Police Manifesto

We believe traders rarely fail because they lack information. They fail because they struggle to execute their own rules consistently.

Trade Police exists to close that gap.

We will not celebrate more trades. We will celebrate better decisions.

We will not hide behind unexplained AI outputs. We will show the evidence.

We will not expose customer strategies to staff who do not need them.

We will not confuse growth with feature count.

We will build the operating system for disciplined traders—one trusted decision at a time.
